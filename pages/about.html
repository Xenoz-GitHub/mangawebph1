About MangaWebPH

Your ultimate destination for manga reading and community

Our Story

MangaWebPH was born from a simple idea: to create the best manga reading experience for Filipino manga fans. Founded in 2026 by a passionate manga enthusiasts, we set out to build a platform that combines a vast library of titles with a vibrant community.

What started as a small project has grown into one of the most popular manga reading platforms in the Philippines, serving millions of readers every month. We're proud to provide a space where manga lovers can discover new series, discuss their favorites, and connect with like-minded fans.

Our mission is to make manga accessible to everyone while supporting the creators who bring these amazing stories to life. We believe in the power of storytelling to inspire, entertain, and bring people together.

Our Core Values

Passion for Manga

We're readers first. Every feature we build starts with the question "Would I want this as a reader?"

Community First

We believe manga is better together. Our community features are designed to bring readers closer.

Innovation

We're constantly improving and adding new features to enhance your reading experience.

Accessibility

Manga should be for everyone. We're committed to keeping our platform free and accessible.
